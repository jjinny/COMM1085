Week 15 Introduction

Congratulations, you’ve almost completed your work in COMM1085. This week, you’ll complete the sole remaining assignment in this course: Part Two of the Final Assignment.

Remember, you’ve already prepared for this assignment in the previous week: completing the review questions, reading the assigned text, and, now that you have submitted Part One of the Final Assignment, analyzing and summarizing the text.

Now you are ready to combine your reading and writing skills to discuss the assigned text by responding to one of the three prompts presented to you in the following assignment.

Good luck!
