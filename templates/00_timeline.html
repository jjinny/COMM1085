Summary Writing – Part 2

How to Write a Summary

The process of writing an effective summary actually begins with effective reading! When we look over what we need to summarize, we look for:

  • Bibliographic information, including title, author, date, and publication information
  • The author’s purpose
  • The main idea and key supporting points

If you need to review this skill of scanning a text, go to {insert quicklink to topic: How to Scan a Text in week 1}.
