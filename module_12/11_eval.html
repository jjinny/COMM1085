This assignment is an opportunity for you to organize your ideas and supporting research for your research project.

Rationale

Your purpose in this task is to develop a comprehensive outline of your research paper, which will include

  • Employing critical thinking to analyse source, voice, bias, meaning, argument, and evidence
  • Applying the three-step process (plan, write, revise) to create a variety of academic and workplace documents
  • Integrating sources using appropriate documentation format
