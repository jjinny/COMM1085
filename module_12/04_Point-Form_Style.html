Using Point-Form Style

Point-form style varies from the style that you will use for writing your final paper. Which characteristics of point-form style can you spot in the following sample?

A. Reasons Krispy Kreme’s First expansion into Canada Failed

Reason #1: High Costs

Topic Sentence: Krispy Kreme’s First attempt to expand into Canada failed due to unexpectedly high costs
Explanation, proof, or example:

  • Stores were too expensive to operate → high upkeep and real estate costs
  • Stores were too big → around 5000 square feet each (Harris, 2016)
  • Stores were too expensive → cost up to 2-3 million dollars to open (Shaw, 2016).

Reason #2: Poor Sales

Topic Sentence: Krispy Kreme’s first attempt to expand into Canada failed due to sales peaking early, resulting in overproduction amid rapid expansion.
Explanation, proof, or example:

  • Sales of donuts were high at beginning → not enough to justify costs over time (Wright, 2017).
  • Sales declined after its first few years in Canada.
