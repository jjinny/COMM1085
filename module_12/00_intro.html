Organizing Research — Part 2

Week 12 Introduction

This week your focus remains on the development of your research paper. At the end of the week, you will submit your Comprehensive Outline, which should comprise all of the information and ideas that you will need to produce your finish draft.

The activities this week are designed to guide you as continue to organize your research and produce an effective outline.

  1. Point-form paraphrasing
  2. Integrating research / text references
  3. Complete outline

Learning Outcomes

Learning Outcomes

Throughout this chapter, you are building the skills to:

  • 2.2 Identify rhetorical patterns and apply them to different writing tasks.
  • 4.5 Use transitions to create unity and coherence in paragraphs.
  • 6.1 Apply the research process to write for workplace and academic purposes.
  • 6.4 Produce an outline that is unified, organized, and coherent.
  • 6.5 Apply an appropriate style of documentation to avoid plagiarism.
