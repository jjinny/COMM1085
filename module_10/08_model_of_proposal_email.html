Good morning Professor Lehrer,

The purpose of this email is to propose to you my ideas for researching Krispy Kreme’s failed Canadian expansion. Please review my ideas, and if satisfactory, allow me to pursue my topic of choice for the final project of COMM1085.

As a student in supply chain management, I would like to understand the decisions, challenges, and obstacles that American companies face when they attempt to expand in Canada. To pursue this interest, I propose to answer the following research question: Will Krispy Kreme’s new expansion into the Canadian market be profitable enough to allow the business to survive long-term in Canada?

I plan to answer this question by writing an analytical report analyzing Krispy Kreme’s new business plan for Canada, why its old business plan failed, and how other similar businesses have survived and thrived in Canada — the most competitive doughnut market in the world.

The LRC has some useful articles from sources such as Canadian Business and the CBC, as well as a detailed article from the Toronto Star about the fierce competition that Krispy Kreme faces in the Canadian doughnut market.
