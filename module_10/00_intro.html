Proposing Research

Week 10 Introduction

This week, our work will focus on the development of your research project. You will develop your interest in particular subject until it is a focused research question. Then, you will present this question to your instructor in the form of a proposal email. A large part of our work this week will be spent looking closely at the expectations of the proposal email assignment.

Learning Outcomes

Learning Outcomes

Throughout this chapter, you are building the skills to:

  • 1.4 Determine purpose and audience when writing
  • 1.5 Write effectively in response to a prompt
  • 2.4 Use appropriate tone and style for the audience and purpose
  • 4.1 Apply the three-step writing process (plan, write, revise)
  • 4.3 Create academic and workplace documents
