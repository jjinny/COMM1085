From Words to Sentences

Now that we are familiar with the parts of speech, we can examine how words combine to form sentences in English. Understanding parts of speech isn’t just a matter of memorizing what category each word belongs to. Instead, it is about understanding how words are being used in a sentence.

Learner Tip

Some words in English can belong to more than one part of speech, depending on how it is being used in a sentence.

Consider the word ‘run’:

  • It can be a noun: Let’s go for a run.
  • It can also be a verb: I run every day.
