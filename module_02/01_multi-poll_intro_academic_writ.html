As you can see, there are opportunities to use reading and writing skills in a variety of contexts, both inside and outside the classroom.

However, our work in this course will focus primarily on reading and writing for an academic or professional context. What does academic writing mean?

To begin, let’s return to the ideas of audience and purpose that we explored last week: what are we trying to do when we write “academically” and who are we writing for when we do it? Answering these questions will help us to prepare for our first writing assignment: summary writing, which we will discuss briefly this week and explore more thoroughly next week.
