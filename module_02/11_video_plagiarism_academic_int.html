VideoPlus: Plagiarism and Academic Integrity

We sometimes use the term “academic integrity” to discuss the standards of honesty and originality that students are expected to meet in their academic work. When student work falls short of these standards, as it can in a variety of ways, this behavior may be described as "plagiarism".

However, "plagiarism" is a complicated and often misunderstood term. Let’s take a closer look at the topic of "academic integrity" so we can better understand these guidelines.

First, watch this video to get a sense of the basic principles of academic integrity. As you watch, the video will pause to present you with statements. Identify whether each statement is true or false.
