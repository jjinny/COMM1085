Summary Writing — Part 1

Activity: Parts of Speech

When we learn about grammar, it is important that we start with the eight parts of speech because they are the building blocks of everything else we will learn about grammar in the coming weeks.

Some of these terms may be familiar, while others are new. But the more you use these terms correctly, the more you will recognize them and apply them correctly. Using grammar is a skill – everyone can learn it, practice it, and improve it in their academic and everyday life.
