Activity: Punctuation

This week we will take a deeper look at punctuation. Our focus will be on the four major punctuation tools: commas, semi-colons, colons, and apostrophes. While other forms of punctuation — such as hyphens, parentheses, and quotation marks — also add meaning and clarity to our writing, the four we will focus on this week are likely to have the greatest impact on the development of your writing skills.

Watch this video about the UK’s “grammar vigilante”. How would you describe what he’s doing? — Is it vandalism or is he a grammar hero? It may seem like a case of grammar pickiness on steroids, but the ambiguity created by the use of a comma was recently cited as the deciding factor in a lawsuit.
