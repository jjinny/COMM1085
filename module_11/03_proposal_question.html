Organizing Research — Part 1

Activity: Proposal Question to Thesis Statement

Now let’s explore the relationship between the proposal question and the thesis statement. You want to make sure that your thesis statement meets these criteria:

  • Identifies your position on the topic
  • Presents your position as an idea that can be challenged and/or needs to be defended
  • Presents your position in clear and specific language
