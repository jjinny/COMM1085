Introduction to College Reading and Writing

Topics and Thesis

Understanding “purpose” is important because it allows us to understand what a text is trying to do and, ultimately, to assess whether it’s effective in achieving its purpose.

Topics

What is the text “about?” What issue does it focus on?

The word ‘topic’ comes from the ancient Greek word topos, which means “place”. Therefore, we can think of the topic of a text as a location for a discussion.

Thesis

What is the author’s position on an issue? What is her interpretation of the topic?

A thesis is also referred to as an “argument,” an opinion that needs to be defended by logic, supported by evidence, and illustrated with examples.
