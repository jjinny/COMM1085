Completing the Research Process — Part 1

Checklist for Research Paper

    Audience and purpose


    • Target audience and purpose are considered
    • The purpose of the research is clear

    Introduction


    • Strong attention grabber is included
    • Issue is clearly introduced
    • Thesis statement or statement of purpose is clear and focused

    Body


    • Clear topic sentence is used for each point of argument
    • At least one paragraph is used for each point
    • Examples, facts, and expert opinions are used to support each point
    • Each paragraph is unified and coherent

    Conclusion


    • Closing provides a final focus
    • Closing reminds readers of the important points presented in the research

    Citation and referencing


    • In-text citations are correctly placed and formatted
    • Reference page is complete and correctly formatted

    Revision


    • Writing is fully revised for structure, tone, grammar, and punctuation

    Feedback


    • Feedback from previous assignments (proposal and outline) has been reviewed and applied
