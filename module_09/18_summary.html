Introduction to Research

Week 09 Summary

This week, we explored two different skills: reflection and research.

1 Reflection:

  • We discussed the value of goal-setting and self-assessment in improving our writing
  • We reviewed three different models for reflection
    • What? So what? Now what?
    • Who? What? Why? Where? When? How?
    • Developmental: Remembering> Understanding>Analyzing>Evaluating> Editing

2 Research:

  • We discussed the differences between library databases and Google that make databases more useful for post-secondary research:
    • detailed info for describing reliability and relevance
    • free access to full texts
    • reliability
    • the ability to sort by relevance and source type
  • We looked at the different kinds of sources found in the databases
  • We looked at the different kinds of websites we can encounter in a Google search and discussed how the type of website affects its reliability
  • We considered strategies for using the databases effectively:
    • preparing a research question
    • doing keyword searches
    • finding specific databases
    • using advanced search tools
  • CARS: Credibility, Accuracy, Relevance, Support
