Introduction to Research

Week 09 Introduction

We are at the mid-point in the semester, and we will use this time both to look back at the work we’ve already completed and to look ahead to the research project that will comprise the majority of our second-half work.

First, we’ll complete the “Feedback Journal” assignment, which asks you to reflect upon the feedback you’ve received on previous assignments. This focus on reflective writing will add another set of tools to your writing toolbox while providing you with an opportunity to build on the lessons you’ve learned up to this point.

Then, our focus will turn to research as we begin preliminary work on the research paper assignment that you’ll develop in several stages over the next few weeks.

Learning Outcomes

Learning Outcomes

Throughout this chapter, you are building the skills to:

  • 2.6 Develop writing skills by incorporating feedback from others
  • 6.1 Apply the research process to write for workplace and academic purposes
  • 6.2 Use the internet and libraries to conduct research
  • 6.3 Use critical thinking skills to gather and evaluate sources to support a thesis or argument
