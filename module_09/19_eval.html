This assignment is an opportunity for you to read, track, and respond to the feedback you receive on your written assignments, including end comments, marginal comments, and rubrics.

This is an individual assignment.

Rationale

This assignment allows you to see writing as a process and to notice improvement in your writing as we move through the course.

  • Employ critical thinking to analyse source, voice, bias, meaning, argument, and evidence

Directions

    1Review content on how to write a Feedback Journal and use the Feedback Journal Template

    2Your feedback journal is divided into two parts:

    • a summary of your professor’s feedback on two assignments from the first half of the semester
    • a plan of action for improvement going forward.

    3Compose a careful written response for each section.

    • Your summary must show that you understand the feedback from your professor.
    • Your plan of action must explain how you will use this feedback to develop your writing skills.

    4Be sure to write in full sentences. Aim for 2-3 short paragraphs per section.

    • You must demonstrate that you have carefully read, understood, and responded to the feedback on your assignments.

    5Use APA@Conestoga format for your analysis:

    • Use 12-point Arial font.
    • Double space your text.

    6Revise your draft to ensure it reads clearly and that it follows the rubric, the assignment instructions, and the resources provided in class.
