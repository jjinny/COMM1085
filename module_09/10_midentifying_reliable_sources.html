Library Resources

Materials that you acquire through the library databases can be considered reliable and trustworthy, as they have all gone through various processes of editing and selection. However, it is important still to understand what kind of text you are reading in order to evaluate how relevant or useful the material is for you own purpose.

Wikipedia

As you likely already know, Wikipedia is a widely-used, free, online encyclopedia that is produced crowd-sourced by volunteer contributors. Although these are monitored and reviewed by senior editors to ensure they follow the site’s standards and policies, academic institutions generally reject Wikipedia as a legitimate source of information.

It’s certainly a contentious topic throughout the academic world, with disagreement among and within professors and students.
